Festive Cursor Trail WordPress Plugin is developed to decorate your blog with new cursor trail. Most of the bloggers or non bloggers wants to decorate their blog on festive season like New Year, Christmas, Halloween, Diwali, Holi, Eid..etc. They may use this plugin for decoration, It also provide option of Share Social Media buttons and Decorative Lights.
Belongs to any country, this plugin doesn't separate the peoples, just type your text to show trail on mouse, Enable disable plugin for your blog.
